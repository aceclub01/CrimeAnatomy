Romance scams exploit emotional vulnerabilities through fake online relationships. Scammers typically:

1. Create compelling fake profiles using stolen photos
2. Establish quick emotional connections ("love bombing")
3. Develop trust through constant communication
4. Fabricate emergencies requiring financial help
5. Disappear after receiving money

Common victim profiles:
- Individuals seeking companionship online
- Those recently divorced or widowed
- People with romantic ideals about "saving" a partner

Psychological triggers exploited:
✓ Loneliness and isolation
✓ Desire for connection
✓ Cognitive dissonance
✓ Romantic idealism

Key prevention strategies:
• Never send money to someone you haven't met in person
• Verify identities through video calls
• Be wary of quick declarations of love
• Consult trusted friends about the relationship
